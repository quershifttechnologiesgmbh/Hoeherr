Training Configuration

Model: YOLOv8m (25.9M params) Epochs: 50 (patience: 10) Batch size: 8 Image size: 640px Optimizer: AdamW (lr=0.001, cosine annealing) Freeze: First 10 layers (backbone) Augmentation: mosaic=1.0, mixup=0.1, degrees=180, HSV shifts Workers: 4 GPU: NVIDIA A100-SXM4-40GB Train time: ~2.3 hours (8,428 seconds)

Key Observations

  • Player detection is reasonable (0.664 mAP@50) but has room for improvement
  • Ball detection is very poor (0.059 mAP@50) - balls are tiny (~6-7px) at drone altitude
  • Training converged around epoch 40 with no significant improvement after
  • Frozen backbone may limit feature adaptation to drone perspective
  • Only SoccerTrack data used - limited diversity

Per-Class Detection Analysis

Player Detection Challenges

  • Players at 50m altitude appear as 15-40px blobs
  • Occlusion during set pieces and tight formations
  • Drone motion blur at edges of frame
  • Shadow confusion (long shadows at low sun angles)

Ball Detection Challenges

  • Ball is only ~6-7px at drone altitude (extremely small)
  • Often occluded by players' bodies
  • Motion blur when kicked at high speed
  • Visually similar to field markings/debris
  • SAHI sliced inference may help significantly

SAHI (Sliced Aided Hyper Inference)

SAHI addresses tiny object detection by slicing high-resolution images into overlapping patches, running detection on each patch, then merging results with NMS post-processing.

How SAHI Works

Input: 3840x2160 frame (or 6500x1000 panoramic) Step 1: Slice into 640x640 patches with 20% overlap (128px) Step 2: Run YOLOv8 on each patch (ball appears ~40-50px in patch = detectable) Step 3: Map detections back to original coordinates Step 4: NMS to remove duplicates at patch boundaries Output: Combined detections

Expected Impact

  • Ball detection: Major improvement expected - ball occupies ~1% of 640px patch vs ~0.1% of full frame
  • Player detection: Moderate improvement - players are already detectable at 640px but edges improve
  • Speed trade-off: ~6-12x slower (number of patches) but can be parallelized

Configuration

slice_height: 640 slice_width: 640 overlap: 0.2 (20%) postprocess: NMS (threshold=0.5) model_type: yolov8
